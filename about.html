About Us

Rent-A-Vec or Rent a vehicle generally serve customers who need a temporary vehicle, such as those who do not own a car, out-of-town travelers, or owners of damaged or destroyed automobiles who are awaiting repair or insurance settlement. Rent-a-Vec also serve the needs of an individual for private transportation in the environment by renting vehicles such as cars and motorcycles.

The cost may vary depending on which vehicle you are taking. You rent it for a minimum of 6hrs and maximum of 4days, you have to pay additional 10% fee per exceeding hours. Gas not included
